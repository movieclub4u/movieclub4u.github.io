Inhuman Kiss

Directed by Sitisiri Mongkolsiri
Screenplay by Chukiat Sakveerakul
Story by Sitisiri Mongkolsiri
Starring Phantira Pipityakorn, Oabnithi Wiwattanawarang, Sapol Assawamunkong, Surasak Wongthai
Running time 122 minutes
Country Thailand
Language Thai
Release date 14 March 2019

Inhuman Kiss also known as Krasue: Inhuman Kiss, is a 2019 Thai romantic horror film directed by Sitisiri Mongkolsiri. It was selected as the Thai entry for the Best International Feature Film at the 92nd Academy Awards, but was not nominated. An innocent girl inherits a curse that causes her head to detach from her body at night to hunt for human flesh.
